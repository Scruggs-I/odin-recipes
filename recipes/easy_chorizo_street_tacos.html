Easy Chorizo Street Tacos

These chorizo tacos use just 5 ingredients and taste amazing. This recipe makes it quick and easy to have a perfect dish for the entire family and a great on-the-go meal for meal prep.

  • Chorizo sausage link (casing removed and meat crumbled)
  • Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce - 2 Tbsp
  • Corn tortillas - 4
  • Chopped onion - 2 Tbsp
  • Fresh cilantro - 2 Tbsp
